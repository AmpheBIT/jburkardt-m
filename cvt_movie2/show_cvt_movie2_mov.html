SHOW_CVT_MOVIE2_MOV

Here is an MOV movie, created by the CVT_MOVIE2 program, showing the evolution of the points in a CVT iteration. In this animation, we are allowing 1 "rebirth" on every step, that is, one generator "dies" and is "reborn" elsewhere.

If your browser won't display the movie, perhaps you can download cvt_movie2.mov to your machine and THEN display it.
