SHOW_CVT_MOVIE_P08_MP4

Here is an MPEG-4 movie, created by the CVT_MOVIE3 program, showing the evolution of the points in a CVT iteration.

The command which created this movie was

cvt_movie3 400 100000 100
(400 nodes, 100000 sample points, 100 frames).

If your browser won't display the movie, perhaps you can download cvt_movie_p08.mp4 to your machine and THEN display it.
